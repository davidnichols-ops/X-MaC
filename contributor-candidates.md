## How to Reach Out (the right way)

**Do:**
- Open a thoughtful issue or discussion on their repo first
- Mention X-MaC naturally in context (e.g., "I'm building something similar")
- Invite them to check out the project, not to work on it
- Star their work, comment on their PRs — build a relationship first
- Use GitHub Discussions if your repo has them

**Don't:**
- Mass-email people (GitHub ToS violation, spam)
- Use emails found on profiles for cold outreach
- Copy-paste the same message to everyone
- Open issues on their repos just to promote X-MaC

**Best approach:** Enable GitHub Discussions on X-MaC, then engage with these developers' projects genuinely. When the time feels right, mention you'd welcome their input on X-MaC.
