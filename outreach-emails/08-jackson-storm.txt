Hi Evgeniy,

DynamicNotch is a really creative use of the MacBook notch — turning it into a living system surface is the kind of thinking that makes macOS development exciting. Impressive work, especially at 19.

I'm building X-MaC, an open source macOS diagnostic and maintenance tool. It has a Rust core for system scanning (disk usage, cache bloat, PATH conflicts, broken symlinks, missing dylibs, environment mapping) and a SwiftUI frontend with a custom neural-network-themed UI. It also includes a GNN model (CoreML) for filesystem anomaly detection.

As a fellow macOS developer, I'd really value your perspective on the project:

https://github.com/davidnichols-ops/X-MaC

Discussions are enabled. If the project interests you, I'd welcome contributions or collaboration of any kind — there's a lot of room for creative UI work.
